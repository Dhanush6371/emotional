## 3. 7 PILLARS OF SELF PROGRAM

**URL:** `https://efitacademy.ca/fundamentally-me-7-pillars-of-self/`

### Program Title
"Fundamentally Me: 7 Pillars of Self"

### Subtitle
"This 7 Step 'To Help Me Help Myself' Program - Gateway to balanced, vibrant existence"

### The 7 Pillars (Core Framework)

1. **Emotional** - What is my heart trying to tell me that I haven't paused to hear?
2. **Purpose** - What deeper purpose is calling me forward in this season?
3. **Financial** - How does my relationship with money reflect how I value myself?
4. **Physical** - In what ways is my body asking for more care, presence, or compassion?
5. **Mental** - How do I respond when life challenges my peace of mind?
6. **Environmental** - What environments and traditions help me feel grounded?
7. **Spiritual** - What practices nourish my spirit and help me stay aligned?

### Program Format
- **Duration:** 10 weeks
- **Format:** Group program
- 10 weekly group sessions
- Pre-program discovery consultation
- Facilitated exploration of each pillar
- Reflective tools, worksheets, pillar-based practices
- Two individual sessions with instructor
- Email/voice note check-ins

### Outcomes
- Practical tools to regulate and respond with clarity
- Deeper sense of purpose and alignment
- Healthier beliefs around money, self-worth, abundance
- Greater connection to body, energy, physical well-being
- Mindset shifts supporting resilience, confidence, self-leadership
- Spiritual foundation anchoring through change and growth

**URL:** `https://efitacademy.ca/group-training-program/`

### Program Title
"Emotional Fitness® Coach Training Course"

### Subtitle
"A modular Emotional Fitness® journey—designed for personal evolution or 
professional development, with certification options available"

### Program Details
- **Duration:** 122 hours (modular format)
- **Format:** Flexible (in-person, virtual, or hybrid)
- Certification pathway available

### The 9 Steps to Emotional Fitness®

1. **Listening Power** - Five stages of powerful listening for better relationships
2. **Learning From Experience** - Review experiences to develop learning opportunities
3. **Lifescale** - Ten questions to discover greater balance
4. **Time Capsule** - Connect present patterns to previous experiences
5. **Group Dialogue** - Seven-stage process for group listening and learning
6. **Storytelling** - Create and learn from your own life stories
7. **Dreamtime** - Step-by-step personal dream interpretation process
8. **The Mirror** - Practice of true self-acknowledgment
9. **Connections** - Use personal symbols for deeper level connections

### Who It's For
- Wellness professionals pursuing certification
- Leaders and changemakers expanding listening capacity
- Support workers and caregivers preventing burnout
- Teams seeking deeper cohesion and connection
- Individuals navigating personal growth or major life transitions

### What's Included
- Pre-program discovery consultation
- Training in flexible modules
- Emotional Fitness® framework and 9 listening tools
- Three individual sessions with instructor
- Ongoing support via email or voice note
- Code of Professional Conduct & Ethics adherence
- Customized workshops & masterclasses (upon request)

### Outcomes
- Certification-ready training hours
- Deepened emotional awareness and practical resilience tools
- Increased confidence, clarity, and personal integrity
- Stronger relationships and team communication
- Skills to support others through challenge and growth
- Foundation for ongoing self-leadership and service
